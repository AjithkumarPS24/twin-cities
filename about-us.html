About us

OUR MANIFESTO

The Heart of What We Do

  • At our core, we believe that healing begins with human connection. In a world of sterile waiting rooms, rushed appointments and overburdened care teams, we are creating something different—a cardiovascular clinic where every heartbeat matters, and every person is seen, heard, and valued.
  • We are not just in the business of treating hearts—we are in the business of caring for people. That means listening deeply, building trust, and forming relationships that last far beyond a single visit. Our care is personal, not procedural.
  • We pair this human-first philosophy with smart, data-driven medicine. Our decisions are guided by the latest science and technology but always filtered through the lens of empathy and experience. We believe that intelligence and intuition are not opposites—they are partners in care.
  • Our space reflects our values. You won’t find cold, industrial corridors or fluorescent lights here. Instead, you’ll walk into a place that feels warm, cozy, and calm—a space designed to put you at ease, to invite conversation, and to support healing from the moment you arrive.
  • We are building a new kind of clinic—one where business is thoughtful, medicine is personal, and care is deeply human.
  • Because at the end of the day, it’s not just about hearts. It’s about the people who carry them.

OUR FOUNDER

THE WHY

  • It takes a village.
  • After practicing cardiology for over 10 years, I’ve come to know the doctors who truly stand out—those with exceptional bedside manner, sharp clinical minds, and a rare ability to heal.These are the same doctors I trust with my own family. They’re in my corner, just a phone call away.
  • But this level of care shouldn’t be a privilege.In today’s healthcare system, the noise of modern medicine has made real connection harder to find.That’s why we reimagined care.
  • At Twin Cities Cardiology, we’ve built something different—a place where excellence is a given, but empathy, time, and trust are what make the experience truly exceptional.This is care as it should be: personal, thoughtful, and deeply human.

  • Dr. Lucy Esberg is a board-certified Cardiologist who has been practicing cardiology since 2013. After earning a Bachelor's degree in Chemistry, Dr. Esberg graduated from Creighton University School of Medicine in 2007. She then completed both her Internal Medicine residency and Cardiology Fellowship at the University of Colorado in 2013.
  • After starting private practice at Aurora Denver Cardiology Associates, Dr. Esberg moved to Minneapolis, where she has spent the past nine years at North Memorial Health, a Level 1 trauma center in Robbinsdale, MN.
    • Dr. Esberg is board-certified in:
    • Cardiovascular Disease
    • Echocardiography
    • Vascular Medicine
    • Vascular Imaging
  • She also served as Chief of Medicine for North Memorial Health for four consecutive years. During this time, she was the Director of the Heart Center’s Vascular Lab and the Director of the Vein Center.
  • In June 2024, Dr. Esberg founded Executive Cardiology Independent Physicians, a concierge cardiology practice. She is now opening Twin Cities Cardiology , a tradition- and evidence-based private practice.
  • Her peers have voted her Minnesota Monthly’s Top Doctor for the past four consecutive years.
