About us

OUR MANIFESTO

The Heart of What We Do

  • We believe healing begins with connection. In a world of rushed visits and impersonal systems, we are creating something different – a cardiovascular clinic where every heartbeat matters, and every person is seen, heard, and valued.
  • Here, care is personal, not procedural. We listen deeply, build trust, and form relationships that last far beyond a single visit. We combine the best of science and technology with the human touch – because we believe intelligence and empathy are partners in care.
  • We designed our space to reflect our values: warm, calm, comforting. From the moment you walk in, we want you to feel at ease.
  • We are building a new kind of clinic – because at the end of the day, it’s not just about hearts. It’s about the people who carry them.

OUR FOUNDER

The Why

  • It takes a village.
  • After over a decade in cardiology, I’ve learned who the truly exceptional doctors are—those with sharp minds, kind hearts, and a real gift for healing. They’re the ones I trust for myself and my family. Just a call away.
  • But I’ve also seen how hard it is for most people to reach this level of care. Modern medicine has created distance. So, I set out to build something better.
  • This clinic “ Twin Cities Cardiology” is the result: a place where medical excellence is given, but where what truly matters is how we show up – for our patients and for each other. A place where care feels like it should.
  • Because healing doesn’t happen in isolation. It happens in connection.
- Lucy Esberg, MD
